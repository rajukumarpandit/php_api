The Indian Premier League (IPL) 2022 was the 15th season of the IPL, a professional Twenty20 cricket league in India. It was held from April 9 to May 29, 2022, and a total of 8 teams participated in the tournament.

The tournament was played across six venues in India and was played in a bio-secure bubble due to the ongoing COVID-19 pandemic. The tournament format remained unchanged, with each team playing 14 matches in a double round-robin format, and the top four teams qualifying for the playoffs. In the final, the Chennai Super Kings defeated the Delhi Capitals by 8 wickets to lift the IPL trophy for the third time. Suresh Raina was named the Player of the Tournament, scoring 548 runs in 14 innings at an average of 49.81 and a strike rate of 135.94.

CricketData followed the entire tournament in Real Time – in our widgets and API both. We’ve had superb responses from our users for this timeframe and Fantasy cricket platforms made a substantial revenue thanks to our dedicated efforts. The IPL 2022 season saw some exciting performances from some of the top players in the world, with a number of records being broken. Kings XI Punjab opener KL Rahul was the leading run-scorer of the tournament, amassing 758 runs in 14 innings at an average of 54.14 and a strike rate of 131.74. In the bowling department, Delhi Capitals pacer Avesh Khan was the leading wicket-taker, claiming 25 wickets in 14 innings at an average of 18.64 and an economy rate of 7.26.

Overall, IPL 2022 with CricketData was a highly successful and entertaining tournament, with many memorable moments and high-quality cricket on display.
